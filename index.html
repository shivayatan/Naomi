USB Port Colors

What do the different colors of USB ports mean?

Click to reveal
ANSWER
White — USB 1.0 (very slow)
Black — USB 2.0 (480 Mbps)
Blue — USB 3.0 / 3.1 (5 Gbps)
Teal — USB 3.2 10Gbps
Red/Yellow — Charging ports
Note: Some manufacturers don't follow the color code.

CPU Socket (AM5)

Where the Ryzen 7 9800X3D is installed.

VRM

Delivers stable power to the CPU. Higher quality = better overclocking and stability.

RAM Slots

Where memory modules are installed. Your board has 2 slots (Mini-ITX limitation).

M.2 Slots

For NVMe SSDs. Your B850I supports fast PCIe 5.0 storage.

PCIe Slot

For your RTX 5080. Runs at full PCIe 5.0 x16 speed.

Arctic AIO All-in-One Cable

Where do I plug the All-in-One cable from the Arctic cooler?

Click to reveal
CONNECTION GUIDE
Main Connection

Plug the thick All-in-One cable into the AIO_PUMP header on the motherboard.

Location on Your Board

Look at the bottom edge of your MSI B850I motherboard (near the 24-pin power connector and front panel headers). The AIO_PUMP header is usually labeled clearly.

Tip: On your motherboard photo, it's the 4-pin header near the bottom center/right area, close to the front panel connectors.

Use the All-in-One cable for simplicity on your first build.
