USB Port Colors

What do the different colors of USB ports mean?

Click to reveal
ANSWER
White — USB 1.0 (very slow)
Black — USB 2.0 (480 Mbps)
Blue — USB 3.0 / 3.1 (5 Gbps)
Teal — USB 3.2 10Gbps
Red/Yellow — Charging ports
Note: Some manufacturers don't follow the color code.

Motherboard Components

What are the main components on a motherboard and what do they do?

Click to reveal
KEY COMPONENTS
CPU Socket

Where the processor is installed. Your board uses AM5 socket for Ryzen 9000 series.

VRM (Voltage Regulator Module)

Delivers stable power to the CPU. Higher quality = better overclocking and stability.

RAM Slots (DIMM)

Where memory modules are installed. Your board has 2 slots (Mini-ITX limitation).

M.2 Slots

For NVMe SSDs. Your B850I supports fast PCIe 5.0 storage.

PCIe Slot

For the graphics card. Your board has one PCIe 5.0 x16 slot.

Chipset

Controls communication between components. Yours uses the B850 chipset.

I/O Shield & Rear Ports

Includes USB, HDMI, LAN, WiFi antenna connectors, and audio ports.
